# Jira - Fast Shipping Feature

## Epic: Fast Shipping Channel

### Story Points Estimate: 21

## User Stories

### US-001: Check Fast Shipping Status
**As** a customer
**I want** to check if fast shipping is available and its details
**So that** I can decide to use the service

### US-002: Browse Fast Shipping Products
**As** a customer
**I want** to see only products eligible for fast shipping
**So that** I can shop for quick delivery items

### US-003: Fast Shipping Checkout
**As** a customer
**I want** to place an order with fast shipping
**So that** I receive my items quickly

### US-004: Channel-Based Content
**As** a customer
**I want** the entire app to show only fast-shipping products when I'm in the fast-shipping channel
**So that** I have a consistent experience
